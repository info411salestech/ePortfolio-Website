    School

Though I am a networking emphasis, my schools (and personal projects) have prepared me in various programming aspects of my Computer Science field. The following are the programming languages I have learned and have been using over the years: Java, C#, HTML/CSS, javascript, and SQL.

During my senior year at Mary Hardin-Baylor, I participated in the Microsoft Imagine cup with a group of three other students.

We designed an application that would help infant mortality by tracking and planning the child's medical life through a calendar by tying the child's mother to a medical professional. During this project, I used C# and ASP.NET to make the web application, and SQL server to store the medical information.

This important project not only tought me about great team work practices, but it also taught me about the extensive work it takes to make a full working system. Below is a video of our group and the system we developed.

One of my most recent projects was to create a firewall in a Linux environment with iptables. This project taught me about various Linux code, firewall implementation, and the many testing procedures to make a full proof firewall.
